Personal Experience with AI Image Generation

Using Copilot as the AI image generator

I used Copilot as one of the AI image generating tools. It is very strong oon generating images. I was quite impressed with its capability. I ask Copilot to generate an image which shows family element. It gave me a sweet family image, which sticks to my request.

Some Challenges I Faced

When I tried to generate an image with very specific and niche details, such as a family wearing traditional clothing from a particular culture while holding specific items, the results were not as accurate.

As I asked the AI to generate a family image with 4 members only, but it generate 5 members in the second photo. Beside, I asked the AI to generate a family image in 4 members with one sister, one brother and thier parents. However, it gave out a 5 members family with 3 sibilings and their parents, which is not following my instructions.

Limitations

  1. Quality and Realism: At this moment the generation of image is still very green. Images are not as realistic as painter.
  2. Complexity and Specificity: When I gave highly detailed or niche prompts, AI can struggle to accurately interpret and execute the request. This can result in images that don't fully meet the user's expectation.
